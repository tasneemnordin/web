FOOD

Ayam masak merah is a Malaysian traditional dish. This literally means chicken "cooked-red" in English. It is a casserole of chicken pieces in dried chillies sambal. Pieces of chicken are first fried to a golden brown then slowly braised in a spicy dried chillies and tomato sauce.

Wikipedia, 2019.

Name of dish: Ayam Masak Merah

Place of origin: Malaysia
